About Us

Our story

I have been searching for a standing desk for more than 3 years, I couldn't find one which was affortable, I had to use unstable carbord boxes. I was tired of searching, So I asked my friend to help me to build one and we did it! I started my new standing desk and I loved it.

I figured more people will have faced the same problem as me so we started Prodesk life to help people.
